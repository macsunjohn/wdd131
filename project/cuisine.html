Cuisine of Nigeria

Nigeria offers a rich variety of cuisines, reflecting its diverse ethnic groups and regions. From spicy soups like Egusi and Ogbono, to flavorful rice dishes like Jollof rice and Fried rice, Nigerian cuisine is full of taste, tradition, and history. Street foods such as Suya, Akara, and Puff-Puff are beloved across the country, while regional specialties highlight the unique flavors of each area.
